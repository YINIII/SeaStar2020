
To run the script for PMT, you have to answer 4 questions after you enter start:
1. After how many seconds you want to start the software?
Your answer should be integer only
2. How many seconds you want to record?
Your answer should be integer only
3. What game time do you want? please enter 'number'
Your answer should follow the format as '1000'
4. What file name do you want?
Your answer should follow the format as	'filename'
